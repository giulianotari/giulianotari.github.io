Wok on the Wild Side

Event Invitation

This invitation was designed for the Culinary Arts and Hospitality Studies Department of City College of San Francisco.Every year the department hosts an event honoring their alumni and showcasing all the different restaurants alumni work at.
My concept for this year's invitation was 'food is not made, it's crafted.' In my illustration I wanted to capture the final moment before food is presented to customers. The invitation feels warm and inviting.
